# Nest Revisions

Nest revisions represent **monitoring visits** to a nest. Each revision documents the nest’s status at a specific date and time, allowing you to track its progress throughout the breeding cycle.

Revisions are essential for understanding nest success, failure, and timing of breeding events.

## Creating a Revision

1. Open the **Nests** module.  
2. Tap a nest to open its details.  
3. Go to the **Revisions** tab.  
4. Tap the **+** button.  
5. Fill in:
   - **Nest status** (active or inactive)
   - **Nest stage** (unknown, building, laying, incubation, hatching, nestling, inactive)  
   - **Number of eggs or nestlings** for the nest owner and nidoparasites
   - **Presence of Philornis larvae**
   - **Notes** (behavior, disturbances, predators, etc.)  
6. Save the revision.

Each revision is stored chronologically.

## Editing a Revision

To update a revision:

1. Tap the revision in the list.  
2. Modify the fields.  
3. Save your changes.

Revisions can be edited at any time.

## Adding Images to a Revision

You can attach photos to document nest condition, contents, or habitat.

1. Open the revision.    
2. Tap **+**.  
3. Choose **Camera** or **Gallery**.  
4. Optionally add a description.  
5. Save.

Tap an image to view it in full screen.

## Requirements for Inactivating a Nest

A nest **must have at least one revision** before it can be marked inactive.  
This ensures that every nest has a minimum monitoring record.

## Why Revisions Matter

Revisions help you:

- Track nest progress over time  
- Document breeding success or failure  
- Record changes in clutch size  
- Identify predation or abandonment events  
- Provide structured data for later analysis in Xolmis Desktop
