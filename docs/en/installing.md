# Installing Xolmis Mobile

Xolmis Mobile is available for both **Android** and **iOS/iPadOS** devices through their official app stores. Installation is simple and requires only a compatible device and an internet connection.

## Requirements

### Android

- Android 7.0 or later  
- Access to the **Google Play Store**

### iOS / iPadOS

- iOS 13 or later  
- iPadOS 13 or later  
- Access to the **Apple App Store**

## Install on Android (Google Play Store)

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your device.  
2. Search for **“Xolmis Mobile”**.  
3. Tap the app in the search results.  
4. Tap **Install**.  
5. Once installation is complete, tap **Open** to launch the app.

You can also update the app from the Play Store whenever a new version becomes available.

## Install on iOS/iPadOS (Apple App Store)

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad.  
2. Search for **“Xolmis Mobile”**.  
3. Tap the app in the search results.  
4. Tap **Get**, then **Install**.  
5. After installation, tap **Open** to start using the app.

Updates are delivered automatically through the App Store.

## First Launch

When opening Xolmis Mobile for the first time, the app may request permissions such as:

- **Location** (for POIs and coordinates)  
- **Camera and Photos** (for attaching images)  
- **Notifications** (for timed inventories)

These permissions enable key features of the app and can be adjusted later in your device settings.

## Keeping Xolmis Mobile Updated

To ensure the best performance and access to new features:

- Enable **automatic updates** in your device’s app store settings.  
- Check periodically for updates in the Play Store or App Store.  

New versions may include bug fixes, performance improvements, updated taxonomy, and new functionality.

Installing Xolmis Mobile through the official app stores ensures a secure, reliable, and up‑to‑date experience for all your fieldwork needs.
