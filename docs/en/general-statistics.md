# General Statistics

The **General Statistics** module provides an overview of your entire dataset in Xolmis Mobile. It summarizes information from inventories, nests, specimens, and field journal notes, giving you a broad understanding of your field activity and data collection patterns.

This screen is designed to help you quickly assess sampling effort, species richness, and monitoring coverage across all modules.

## Accessing General Statistics

1. Open the main menu.  
2. Tap **Statistics**.  
3. The General Statistics screen will load automatically.

This module does not require selecting items; it summarizes **all data stored in the app**.

## Summary Metrics

At the top of the screen, you will find key global metrics:

- **Total species recorded** – unique species across all inventories.
- **Total inventories** – number of inventories created.
- **Total nests** – including active and inactive nests.  
- **Total specimens** – pending and archived.  
- **Total POIs recorded** – number of POIs created.
- **Total localities sampled** – number of unique localities across all records.
- **Top 5 species most recorded** – list with the most recorded species across all records, tap **See all** to open the complete list.
- **Records per hour** – histogram showing the distribution of records per hour.
- **Records per month** – histogram showing the distribution of records per month of the year.
- **Species richness per month** – histogram showing the species richness per month of the year.
- **Species richness per year** – histogram showing the species richness per year.

These values provide a quick snapshot of your overall fieldwork activity.

## Inventories

This section shows statistics specific to inventories.

- **Total time sampled** – number of non-overlaping sampling hours of inventories.
- **Average time sampled** – average number of hours used to sample inventories.
- **Total days sampled** – number of days with inventories.
- **Total localities sampled** – number of distinct localities sampled in inventories.

## Nests

This section shows statistics specific to nests.

- **Apparent success rate** – apparent success rate of all nests.
- **Nidoparasitism rate** – rate of nidoparasitism in all nests.

### Nests by Fate

A summary chart shows the distribution of nest outcomes:

- Success
- Lost
- Unknown  

This provides a quick overview of breeding success across all monitored nests.

## Specimens

This section show statistics specific to specimens.

### Specimens by Sample Type

A chart displays the number of specimens collected per sample type:

- Feathers  
- Tissue  
- Carcasses  
- Blood  
- Other materials  

Useful for evaluating the diversity of collected samples.

## When to Use General Statistics

General Statistics is useful when:

- Reviewing overall fieldwork effort  
- Preparing reports or summaries  
- Evaluating long-term sampling patterns  
- Checking data completeness before exporting  
- Planning future field campaigns  

This module provides a high-level overview of your entire dataset, helping you understand your field activity at a glance.
