# Species and Records List

The **species list** (or **records list**, depending on the inventory type) is the core component of every inventory in Xolmis Mobile. It displays all species detected during the survey and provides tools to manage counts, add coordinates, edit notes, and organize your observations efficiently.

This section explains how to use the species list during fieldwork.

## Adding a Species

1. Open an inventory.  
2. Tap the **Search species** field at the top of the list.  
3. Type part of the scientific name.  
   - You may use:
     - Initials of genus + species (e.g., *peob* → *Penelope obscura*)  
     - Partial matches  
     - Multiple fragments separated by spaces  
4. Select the species from the suggestions.

If the species is added after the inventory is finished, it will be marked **outside the sample**.

### Adding a custom species

If the species is not found:

- Tap the **⋮** menu in the search bar  
- Select **Add species**  
- Enter a temporary name  

The custom name will appear exactly as typed.

## Adjusting the Number of Individuals

For quantitative methods (transects, point counts, banding, etc.):

- Use the **+** and **–** buttons next to the species name.  
- Tap the number itself to enter large values manually.  
- Default count is **1** when adding a species.

Qualitative lists do not track individual counts.

## Adding POIs (Points of Interest)

A POI stores the **GPS coordinates** of a species detection.

To add a POI:

1. Tap the **GPS icon** on the right side of the species entry.  
2. The app records the current device location.  

To view or manage POIs:

- Tap the **species name** to open the details screen.  
- Long‑press a POI to:
  - View details  
  - Add notes  
  - Delete the POI  

POIs are included when exporting to **KML**.

## Editing Species Details

Long‑press a species in the list to open the action menu:

- **Details** – Add notes or change the species.  
- **Remove from sample** – Marks the species as recorded outside the sampling period.  
- **Include in sample** – Reverses the above.  
- **Add POI** – Records a new coordinate.  
- **Delete** – Removes the species and all associated POIs.

## Species Inside vs. Outside the Sample

Species added **after finishing** an inventory are marked:

- **Gray**  
- Labeled as **outside sample**

This distinction is important for:

- Mackinnon lists  
- Accumulation curves  
- Statistics  
- Exported reports  

## Detection-Based Methods

For **Detection Transect** and **Detection Point** inventories:

- Each detection is a **separate record**, not a single species entry.  
- After selecting a species, a form opens to fill:
  - Distance  
  - Flight height  
  - Flight direction  
  - Group size  

These inventories allow **multiple entries of the same species**.

## Sorting the Species List

Tap the **sort icon** to choose how the list is ordered.  
Sorting options may include:

- Species (alphabetical)  
- Time added  

Sorting helps during long surveys or when reviewing data.

## More Options (⋮ Menu)

The **⋮ menu** in the search bar provides additional tools:

- **Add species** (custom name)  
- **Species accumulation chart**  

These tools help evaluate sampling completeness and inventory performance.

## Synchronization Between Active Inventories

When multiple inventories are active:

- Adding a species to one inventory **automatically adds it to all others**,  
  except for **Banding** inventories.

This allows:

- A qualitative list to run in parallel with point counts or transects  
- Automatic consolidation of species detected during the session  

## Automatic Feedback and Suggestions

When the species list is empty, the app may show **action suggestions**, such as:

- Add species  

These suggestions help guide new users.

The species/records list is designed to be fast, flexible, and field‑friendly, ensuring that your observations are captured accurately and efficiently during any type of survey.

*[POI]: Point of Interest
